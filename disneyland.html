Disneyland Resort is the largest theme park in the world. It is located in Anaheim, California. There are three parks at Disneyland: Disneyland Park, Disney California Adventure Park, and Disney's Hollywood Studios. Disneyland has hosted more than one million guests each year for more than 50 years.
